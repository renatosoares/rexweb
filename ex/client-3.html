leaDNA Makes Daily Fandemic Fantasy a Reality

Launched in 2013, Daily Fandemic brought fantasy sports to the 21st century. Gone are the days of picking last, bad trades and injuries determining championships. Their innovative Daily Seasonal method is the “Texas Hold’em of fantasy sports”.

Solution & Results

Daily Fandemic had a brilliant & innovative approach and needed to spread the word. Fantasy sports rely on engaged and active players to achieve success. leaDNA provided Daily Fandemic with all the tools to achieve high level brand recognition and exposure.

Utilizing the leaDNA engine a PPC demand-based lead generation campaign took Daily Fandemic to the next level. leaDNA made sure to take advantage of the changing sport seasons to target die-hard fans. Email Campaigns were scheduled throughout the year to stay on track with kick-offs, openers, and starters alike. A large portion of email campaigns were promotional emails to introduce potential members to Daily Fandemic. Social campaigns launched on the leaDNA platform generated high conversion leads. With the help of leaDNA, Daily Fandemic was able to maintain control in the volatile world of sports. Daily Fandemic has reached a high level of exposure and success with over 2,000 new leads generated and counting.
